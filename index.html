About GuessGenie

My passion for merging game mechanics with engaging user experiences sparked the creation of GuessGenie. During a brainstorming session, I envisioned a platform that not only entertains but also challenges users on an intellectual level. GuessGenie embodies my personal exploration into the realm of interactive gaming and user engagement.

The journey began in January 2024. Through iterative design and development phases, GuessGenie evolved into the polished and interactive experience you see today.

GuessGenie serves as a portfolio project for Holberton School, demonstrating the practical application of the skills and knowledge acquired during the course. [Portfolio Project Link](#)
